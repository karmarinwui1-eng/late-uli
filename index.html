is a pixelated game about crossing roads in a rush. Since we want to simulate a game where a student or a person rushes along the streets, in order to get to their destination with obstacles along the way that may vary: Karen running on the road, dog chasing, white van, and etc. We wanted to give our user a toe curling experience, while playing Late-Uli since, as the game progresses goes on the faster will the cars and other obstacles will be, additionally whenever a player collide with a vehicle the game system will give out a reward or punishment effect either on their health, stunned their running, give them speed boost and so forth. Players will be collecting power ups along the way which is spawned by the game system automatically, that they can use to get to the “EJEEP”
